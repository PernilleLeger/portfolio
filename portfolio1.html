ABOUT

My name is Pernille Leger Kristensen. I am 21 years old and I study multimedia design at CPH Business Academy.

After taking courses in Photoshop and InDesign at BMCC in Manhattan I applied to CPH Business so that I could learn more about design, but also to become best friends with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I see myself as a very dedicated student who puts a lot of time, effort and creativity into my projects.

Creating is a passion of mine, whether it is a website, a table or a poster. For me, creativity and curiosity go hand in hand and I always want to learn new things.
